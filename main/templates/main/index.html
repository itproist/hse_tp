How does it work?


Our chat-bot uses artificial intelligence to analyze the ingredients you have. Simply input what's in
your frigde, and it will suggest delliious recipes tailored to those items. It's like having a personal chef
at your fingestips.




What can it do?


The chat-bot can recommend recipes based on the ingredients you provide. It also offers cooking tips
and alternatives for missing items. With endless culinary possibilities, it helps reduce food waste and
inspires creativity in the kitchen.




Is it user-friendly?


Absolutely! The interface is designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate. You can quickly start a
conversation and receive recipe suggestions within moments. It's perfect for both novice cooks and
seasoned chefs,




Can I save recipes?


Yes, you can save your favourite recipes for easy access later. The chat-bot allows you to create a
personal recipe collection, This feature makes meal planning and preparation even more convenient.




Is it free to use?


Yes, our chat-bot is free to use! You can access all its features without any charges. We believe in
making cooking accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
